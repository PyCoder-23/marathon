🏃‍♂️ MARATHON SERVER: USER GUIDE 🏃‍♂️
Welcome to the ultimate consistency training camp. Follow this protocol to link your identity and start your marathon.

🔗 PHASE 1: IDENTITY SYNC
Before you can earn XP or use our tools, you must link your Discord to the platform.

1️⃣ In any channel, type: /link 

2️⃣ Check your DMs for a unique 8-character code. 

3️⃣ Go to the Marathon Website, click "Join Training Camp", and enter your code.

4️⃣ Done! You are now an active Agent, and you will automatically be drafted into one of the four competitive **Squads** (Zenith Sentinels, Apex Titans, Meridian Arbiters, or Horizon Vanguards).

⏱️ PHASE 2: THE BOT (FIELD OPS)
The bot is your primary tool for recording study sessions.

🔹 START SESSION: /timer start — Initializes your focus clock. 
🔹 PAUSE / RESUME: /timer pause | /timer resume 
🔹 END & SUBMIT: /timer end — Finalizes session. (Requires photo proof of your work!). 
🔹 RANK: /rank — See your personal position on the weekly leaderboard. 
🔹 LEADERBOARD: /leaderboard — View the interactive weekly server rankings.
🔹 ACTIVE SESSIONS: /active — Live dashboard of all agents currently working or resting.
🔹 PROFILE: /stalk @user — Check an agent's XP, streak, rank, and squad allegiance.
🔹 SQUAD STATUS: /squad-status — View your squad's current rank, total weekly XP, and your contribution.
🔹 SQUAD LEADERBOARD: /squad-leaderboard — View the live rankings of all four squads.

🏛️ PHASE 3: THE WEB (COMMAND CENTER)
The dashboard is your high-tech HUD for long-term planning.

📅 PLANNER: A 4 AM – 4 AM weekly grid to block out your marathons. 
📝 JOURNAL: A full-screen writing environment with mood tracking. 
🎯 TASKS: Manage your tactical daily objectives. 
🏆 LEADERBOARD: Real-time weekly rankings based on personal XP.
🛡️ SQUADS: View the dynamic Squads leaderboard, check rivalry standings, and see the full active roster for each faction.

🔥 PHASE 4: THE STREAK & XP PROTOCOL
Consistency is our only currency.

RULE: Log at least one study session every day to keep your streak alive.
XP FORMULA: Sessions under 25 minutes yield 0 XP. Beyond 25 minutes, you earn XP proportionally (~4/5 XP for every minute worked. e.g., 45 mins = 36 XP).
GRACE: You have 48 hours to log a new session before your streak resets to 1.
SQUAD CONTRIBUTION: You only contribute to your squad's weekly score if you are an **Active Member** (earning at least 100 XP that week).
WEEKLY RESET: Every week, the personal and squad XP leaderboards reset. Streaks are Permanent. The winning squad is permanently immortalized in the Discord server!

STAY DISCIPLINED. ENJOY THE MARATHON. 🦾🚀
