# 🚀 MARATHON SERVER UPDATES

## Squads System Update: A Much Long-Awaited Feature!

We are incredibly excited to finally roll out the highly anticipated **Squads System**! This massive update transforms Marathon from a solo-grind tracker into a fully-fledged faction-based competition. Agents will now fight not just for personal glory, but for the dominance of their respective Squads.

Here is a detailed breakdown of everything included in today's massive update:

### 🛡️ The Four Squads
Upon linking your Discord account to the Marathon platform, you will automatically be drafted into one of four elite squads. We developed a custom load-balancing algorithm to ensure you are assigned to the squad that currently needs you the most! 
* **The Zenith Sentinels:** Violet & Obsidian. *"None Stand Above."*
* **The Apex Titans:** Cyan & Steel Gray. *"The Peak is Our Domain."*
* **The Meridian Arbiters:** Emerald Green. *"Control the Center, Rule the Field."*
* **The Horizon Vanguards:** Orange & Crimson. *"No Limit. No End."*

Your respective Team leaders have the @Team Leader role, and you can ask admins or team leaders in #requests to change your squad if you have a valid reason (they are the only ones who can change your squad). They will soon create custom squad categories and channels to foster a stronger sense of community within each squad.

### 📊 The Web App: Squads Command Center
We've introduced a brand-new **Squads Portal** to the website navigation:
* **The Overview Leaderboard (`/squads`):** A premium UI that ranks all four squads in real-time. Only active members (who have earned ≥ 100 XP this week) contribute to the weekly standing. Watch your squad's rank dynamically shimmer in Diamond, Gold, Silver, or Bronze.
* **Deep-Dive Squad Profiles (`/squads/[name]`):** Clicking on a squad takes you to their beautifully themed headquarters. These pages feature massive hero background banners, the squad's unique lore/vibe, and a custom UI that automatically adopts the squad's glowing color scheme. It includes a real-time **Rivalry Banner** (showing how far ahead or behind you are to the next squad), an **MVP Showcase**, and a fully detailed **Active Roster** table.
* **Dashboard & Profile Integration:** Your Web Dashboard and public Profile Pages now proudly display your Squad Shield and your specific rank *within* that squad. 

### 🤖 The Bot: Squads Field Ops
The Discord bot has been vastly expanded to support squad warfare:
* **`/squad-status`:** Instantly pull up a customized Discord embed showing your squad's current rank, total weekly XP, and your personal contribution.
* **`/squad-leaderboard`:** View the live rankings of all four squads directly in Discord, fully synced with the database.
* **`/stalk`:** The classic stalk command now reveals an agent's squad allegiance and their rank within their squad.
* **`/set-squad` & `/randomize-squads`:** Powerful new admin tools to manually reassign users or completely randomize and evenly distribute all agents into new squads (automatically handling Discord roles and database syncing simultaneously).

### 🏆 Permanent Legacy (The Weekly Reset)
Squad warfare isn't just about the current week. During the **Weekly Hard Reset Protocol** every Sunday night, the winning squad will be manually announced on Discord and permanently recorded in the respective historical channels for ultimate bragging rights!

### ⏰ Automated Terminations & Reset Reminders
To keep the database clean and ensure agents are utilizing the bot accurately, we have introduced a powerful automated session termination system:
* **Daily Terminations:** Every morning at **4:30 AM IST** (except on Mondays), all active sessions across the server will be automatically terminated. This prevents runaway timers from abandoned sessions.
* **The 🔔 Ping Role:** We've added a new Reaction Role system. By reacting with the 🔔 emoji in the server, you will automatically be assigned the **Reset Reminders** role and receive a confirmation DM. 
* **Early Warnings:** Users with the ping role will receive an automated ping exactly 30 minutes before the 4:30 AM daily reset, as well as critical 30-minute and 10-minute warnings before the Sunday Weekly Hard Reset!

Some Upcoming Features:
1. Custom marathon server currency and shop for winning squads, the currency can be used to redeem rewards such as XP Boosts, profile cosmetics and much more!
2. Members auctioning events for squad leaders to bid on members to join their squad (this will be a VC event and will be a few days in advance)

Stay disciplined, represent your squad, and enjoy the marathon. 🦾🚀
